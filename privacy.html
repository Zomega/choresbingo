Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Our Philosophy

We believe your household data belongs to you. We have designed this app so that we never see, store, or sell your personal information. There is no central database; the "game" exists only between you and your family.

2. Data Handling

Local Storage: Your game state, custom chore names, and scores are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you share it with others.

Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Sync: When you play with others, data is sent directly between devices using encrypted P2P protocols. We do not intercept, log, or even see this traffic.

3. Your Control

Because we do not store your data on our servers, you have total control over it:

  • Transparency: You can view all your data within the app settings.
  • Instant Deletion: You can delete all your data permanently by using the "Delete All Local Data" button in the settings or by clearing your browser cache.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use trackers, cookies for advertising, third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), or any advertising SDKs.
